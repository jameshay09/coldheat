About


Cold Heat are a 5-Piece Alternative Rock band based out of Peterhead in the North East of Scotland. We are influenced by artists such as Kings of Leon, Stereophonics, Nirvana, Elvis Presley, Johnny Cash, Ramones, Led Zeppelin, Foo Fighters, etc.
